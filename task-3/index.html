Blue

Blue is the color of the sky and sea. It is often associated with depth ans stability. It symbolizes trust loyality, wisdom, confidence, intelligence, faith, truth and heaven.

Green

Green is the color of nature. It symbolizes growth, harmony, freshness, and fertillity. Green has strong emotional correspondencs with safety.

Red
